<section class="book-intro">
  <h2>Abstract</h2>
  <p>In the study of power-law graph spectra, two competing approaches exist: Wigner's semicircle law vs power-law eigenvalue distribution. Chung, Lu, and Vu reconcile both by showing the Laplacian follows the semicircle law while the adjacency matrix obeys the power law. Results are based on random graphs with given expected degrees, with phase transitions at <em>β</em> = 2.5.</p>
